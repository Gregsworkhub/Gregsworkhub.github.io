About Me

Hi I'm Greg, an amateur woodworker in the UK who started the CNC journey with a 3018 pro machine, small but useful for learning your way around the world of creating, designing and using G-Code, i'm not a pro (yet!) but i'm learning every day. I've recently progressed to a Shapeoko 4 Pro XXL machine and built a custom workshop, a dedicated CNC room and a work area for finishing and other projects. On this site i will be sharing some of my wins, flops and equipment that i use along the way. i've provide links to where you can find the equipmenti use. Stick around and follow me on my journey or drop me a line if you a fellow CNC newbie, lets make something awesome together.
